Our Impact

Read more about the positive impact we have had on the Peoples Oakland community through browsing testimonials and viewing our outcomes.

I was formally the legal guardian to Joy Becquet for over 30 years. She was a very important person in my life. Sadly, she passed away this last January, 2020. I have so many positive feelings towards Peoples Oakland that I’ve acquired over the many, many years Joy attended the program.

Susan Davis

Guardian of Former Member

Peoples Oakland was my first experience as a social worker and I am so thankful that it was. Both staff and members alike helped me build a strong foundation as an intern that I have been able to apply in everything that I’ve done since leaving. Every time I stepped into the building I learned something new.

Darelle Cowley

Student Intern

They gave me a job here as a receptionist so that made me feel good about myself and that did a lot for me. Through the years I met a lot of wonderful people and learned how to trust again; it gave me confidence and sort of a purpose in life.

Melvin Lee

Current Member

My time at PO was better than I could ever have imagined it would be. Coming in as a new intern from the Pitt Social Work I had no experience in the mental health field, but the staff and members at Peoples Oakland made me feel at home immediately.

Leah Donahue

Student Intern

Outcomes

Peoples Oakland counselors offer crisis intervention services to resolve member crises across a variety of domains. We also conduct annual surveys to ensure that our members feel socially connected and satisfied with the quality of our service.
