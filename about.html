About Us

Peoples Oakland strives to be a premier recovery and wellness center, and is committed to holistic, comprehensive, member driven recovery. Learn more about our history, mission, and vision below.

History

Peoples Oakland is a private non-profit agency established by local residents as a community, planning and organizing agency. Originally, the mission was to enhance the integrity of the community through communications and negotiation among residents, merchants and institutional representatives. Peoples Oakland was very successful in its early organizing efforts, leading to the development of several community wide planning structures which still operate today. The Peoples Oakland Mental Health Program was founded in response to the crisis faced by thousands of state mental hospital patients who, through deinstitutionalization programs, were released into the community with few supporters or social connections. Peoples Oakland began to organize former state hospital patients, providing education to support them in advocating for their rights.

Mission Statement

Peoples Oakland strives to be a premier recovery and wellness center, and is committed to holistic, comprehensive, member driven recovery. Guiding all Peoples Oakland services and activities is a philosophy of Recovery based on real life experiences nurtured by peer support, hope, self-help and collaborative relationships with professionals. Peoples Oakland continues as a leader in providing Recovery based comprehensive psychiatric and social rehabilitation services for Allegheny County adults with severe and persistent mental illness and co-occurring disorders.

Vision

Peoples Oakland will continue to build an organization that, with partners and through networks, will make this region a leader in supporting people who are recovering from serious and persistent mental illness and co-occurring disorders.

Our Values

Social Connectnedness

Social connectedness surveys are administered annually. Findings of a T-test showed a significant increase in social connectedness for members who have attended Peoples Oakland for at least 6 months.

Quality Satisfaction

Members have reported high levels of satisfaction, with 90% of members stating that they enjoy the services that they receive at Peoples Oakland and feel encouraged to actively participate in groups.

Respect

At Peoples Oakland, it is our top priority to treat everyone with a respect and kindness. We want to foster a community of love and acceptance to ensure that everyone feels safe and comfortable.


Services We Provide

The Peoples Oakland Reentry program is an expansion of our services that addresses the needs of individuals suffering from severe mental illness that are being released from the Allegheny County Jail. We will assist these individuals in obtaining appropriate treatment, while providing community support and therapeutic support. For individuals who have recently experienced incarceration, this provides them with a new community and social outlet. By changing their social environment, they are more likely to establish healthy daily rhythms, and will be surrounded by other members who are committed to working towards their own recovery goals.




Counseling and Therapeutic Support

Our Psychiatric Rehabilitation Counselors offer daily crisis intervention, helping to resolve member crises across a variety of domains -- psychological, medical, interpersonal, housing, bereavement, victim or accused perpetrator of a criminal or violent act, and financial. Some additional therapeutic services include:

- Individual Counseling
- Daily Recovery Support Group
- Anger Management
- Mental Illness and Substance Abuse Support Group
- Various Psychoeducational and recovery support groups based on member feedback and therapeutic needs.



Employment Services

Peoples Oakland offers a wide range of employment services and education supports. Supports and opportunities are tailored to each member’s strength and interests. Member’s interest range from entering the work force to pursuing their education by attending local training centers and universities. Services offered include:

- On-the-job training opportunities
- Resume development
- Job search and interview assistance
- Linkage to benefits counseling
- Educational supports and individual computer accounts with internet access



Peer Mentorship Services

Peoples Oakland has always been a leader in self-help and peer support models. Opportunities are available for members to develop, lead and participate in activities and groups; work as a peer counselor; participate in community consumer activities such as CSP and PSAN; attend conferences and workshop.

- Every member assigned to a rehabilitation counselor to assist them in developing their own Recovery plan
- Opportunities to improve physical health and social life through group participation
- Members actively participate in the management of Peoples Oakland by developing and leading groups and providing useful feedback



Social and Recreation Services

Social and Rehabilitation activities provide opportunities to meet and make friends and to participate in fun events at Peoples Oakland and in the community. Activities are continually evolving to meet the interests of the membership. Some social activities include:

- Breakfast Club
- Board Games, Pool, and Ping Pong
- Arts and Crafts Activities
- Bingo and Other Games
- Community Outings



Nutrition and Fitness Services

Nutrition and Fitness activities assist members in understanding and applying a holistic approach to creating a healthy lifestyle. Programs are subject to rotation based on member needs and specific requests.
